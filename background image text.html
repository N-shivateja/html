GOD SHIVA

background text for the image:

Shiva (Siva) is one of the most important gods in
the Hindu pantheon and is considered a
member of the holy trinity (trimurti) of
Hinduism with Brahma and Vishnu. A complex character,
he may represent goodness, benevolence and serve
as the Protector. He is also associated with Time,
and particularly as the destroyer and creator of all things.
